Quad and Glute Exercises

  • Squat
    • How To Properly Set-Up a Back Squat

    Squats are constantly performed incorrectly and can lead to back pain and muscle imbalances if done incorrectly.

    We recommend finding your 1 rep max and put in a working set of 10 reps of 50%, 7 reps of 70% and 5 reps of 80%.

  • Front Squats
    • Front Squat Set-Up

    Front squats are definetly a hard working exercise but they target the quadriceps exceptionally well giving you a more defined leg

    For front squats we recommend to 2 sets of 10 for 65% of your one rep max
