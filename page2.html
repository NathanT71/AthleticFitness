Workout Page

Welcome to the workout page! This guide will help you get started on building your own unique physique.

The key to gaining your ideal physique is lifting weights that you can handle, while ensuring you maintain great technique throughout each exercise.

You also need to progressively overload which is just adding more weight when the weight you are comfortable with seems too easy or light. It also does not have to be a substantial amount of added weight, 5 or 10 pounds is more than enough.
